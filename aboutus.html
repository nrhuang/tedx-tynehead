Who Are We?

We are students from Surrey who are passionate about sharing ideas with the youth around us.

Meet our Speakers!

Speaker 1: Nelli Warren

Bio: "Ms. Nelli Warren is an accomplished educator and English tutor who helps Tri Cities and Fraser Valley high school students (including those in Surrey, Langley, Coquitlam and Port Coquitlam) get better marks in the classroom and succeed on the BC Provincial English Exam. She provides them with skills and techniques that will dramatically increase their confidence in English. She also assists both students and teachers in maximizing their achievements in the areas of writing and analysis."
